Why this blog?!

Some people are super effective. They speak at meetups and conferences, write posts, books, make tutorials, learn things, have a life, do sports. I mean, that’s a lot of stuff! How do they do that? Are they sleeping? Or is it about being motivated and well organized?

Some of my friends and I meet once a month to work together, we are all working in different remote companies. During a coffee break, we were talking about those super-productive people. It was at that moment someone popped up with the Learn in Public method.

Ok … but what the hell is it? In a few words Learn in Public is having the habit to produce public resources about what you have learned. It could be a post, a video, a speech and so on … your choice! In some articles is mentioned one more rule; the audience should be your future self, and in my opinion, this is the most important.

Don’t judge your results by “claps” or retweets or stars or upvotes - just talk to yourself from 3 months ago. - swyx.io/learn-in-public

This is why I created this blog. I want to try this method to help me improve my organization skills and my effectiveness. A public blog for my future self could be the right motivation.

I’m not sure if it is going to work for me, but there is only one way to know; try it and see how it goes!
